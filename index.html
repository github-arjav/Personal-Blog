How to start preparing for interviews 17 June 2022

Learn as much as you can about the company you are interviewing with. Visit their website, read their mission statement, and research their industry. This will help you understand the company's values, culture, and priorities, and help you tailor your responses to their needs. Go over the job description and make sure you understand the key responsibilities, required skills, and qualifications. This will help you prepare examples of your experience and skills that demonstrate you are the right fit for the position.
